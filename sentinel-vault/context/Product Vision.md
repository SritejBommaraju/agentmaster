# Product Vision

**Product:** ExecuSim — AI Market Simulation Executive

## Goal

Simulate → Evaluate → Pivot → Resimulate → Score

ExecuSim lets founders validate B2B ideas in minutes by running a multi-agent simulation: a strategy agent generates an ICP and pitch, synthetic customer personas respond, and an executive supervisor iterates until the idea shows traction.

## Problem It Solves

Founders can't validate B2B ideas quickly:
- Real interviews take weeks
- Outreach requires infrastructure
- Market research is shallow

ExecuSim replaces early-stage validation with a synthetic market of AI customers and an AI executive that iterates until the idea achieves traction.

## Core Value Props

- Instant ICP, pricing, and messaging generation from a one-line idea
- 3–5 synthetic customer personas with realistic objections and willingness scores
- Executive supervisor that pivots strategy and reruns simulation automatically
- Final validation score with weighted persona agreement rates

## Target Context

Built as a demo-ready product. Judged on:
1. User enters idea
2. Strategy and personas generated visibly
3. Simulation runs — objections surface
4. Supervisor pivots pricing/messaging
5. Second simulation shows improved adoption
6. Validation score output (e.g., 72%)

## Pitch

"We simulate a market of AI customers and let an AI executive iterate until the idea shows traction."
